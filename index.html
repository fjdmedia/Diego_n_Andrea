What's a Wedding Social?

A Manitoba tradition

A wedding social is a pre-wedding celebration where friends, family, and the whole community come together to support the couple before their big day.

You buy raffle tickets for a shot at awesome prizes, enjoy a 50/50 draw, grab a drink, eat good food, and dance all night — all to celebrate Diego & Andrea.

Every ticket helps the couple kick off the next chapter. Come ready to win and have a great time.
